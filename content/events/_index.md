## How to Participate in Public Meetings

- **Public comment period:** Most hearings include a public comment period.
  Arrive early to sign up to speak; comments are often limited to **[2-3
  minutes]** per speaker.
- **Written comments:** You can submit written comments before or during a
  hearing — see [Comment Letters](/comment-letters/) for guidance.
- **Can't attend in person?** Many hearings offer remote/virtual participation
  — check the agenda link for dial-in or video details.
