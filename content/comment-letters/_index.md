Public comment letters are one of the most effective tools we have. Agencies are
legally required to respond to substantive comments on the environmental review,
and a strong record of specific, well-documented concerns matters — both for the
current decision and for any future legal challenge.

## Comment Deadline

**[Month Day, Year] at [time], [time zone]** — comments must be received by this
date to be part of the official record. Late comments may still be read but are
not guaranteed a written response.

## How to Submit a Comment

1. **Read the concern area(s)** below that matter most to you — you don't need to
   cover all four.
2. **Personalize a letter.** Use our talking points and template language as a
   starting point, but write in your own words. Personal letters citing specific,
   individual impacts (your street, your commute, your kids' school) carry more
   weight than form letters.
3. **Submit your letter** to:
   - Email: **[agency contact email]**
   - Mail: **[agency mailing address]**
   - Online portal: **[link to submission portal, if any]**
   - In person: at the **[hearing name/date]** (see [Events](/events/))
4. **Cc us** at **[campaign email]** so we can track the volume and content of
   community comments (optional, but helpful).

## Concern Areas

<div class="comment-letter-categories">

### 🚚 [Traffic & Safety](/comment-letters/traffic/)
Truck routes, congestion, pedestrian and cyclist safety, school-zone impacts.

### 🌫️ [Air Quality](/comment-letters/air-quality/)
Diesel particulate matter, emissions modeling, health impacts on nearby residents.

### 🏘️ [Community Character](/comment-letters/community-character/)
Land use compatibility, noise, lighting, visual impacts, property values.

### 📋 [CEQA Compliance](/comment-letters/ceqa/)
Adequacy of the environmental review, alternatives analysis, mitigation measures.

</div>

Each concern area page includes background, suggested talking points, and a
downloadable/copyable template letter you can adapt.
