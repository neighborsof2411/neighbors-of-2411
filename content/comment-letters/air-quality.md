## Background

Warehouse and logistics facilities generate diesel particulate matter (DPM) and
other pollutants primarily from truck traffic, refrigeration units (TRUs), and
backup generators. Nearby sensitive receptors — homes, schools, parks — may face
elevated health risks from long-term exposure.

## Talking Points

- The project's health risk assessment [does / does not] account for
  cumulative impacts from other nearby industrial sources.
- Sensitive receptors including **[school/park/senior housing name]** are
  within **[distance]** of the project site, within the zone of significant
  impact identified by [air district] guidance.
- Request mandatory zero-emission or near-zero-emission truck requirements,
  electrified loading docks, and no on-site diesel backup generators.
- Request real-time air quality monitoring at the property line, with publicly
  posted results, for the life of the project.
- Request idling limits stricter than the state default (currently 5 minutes).
